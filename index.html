What You'll Find

A World of Antiques Under
One Massive Roof

With dozens of vendors, our selection is unmatched. Every vendor brings their own specialty and style, creating a treasure-hunting experience unlike any other antique shop in Orange County.

Multi-Vendor Antique Mall

Dozens of independent vendors in one location. Each vendor curates their own space with unique finds. Explore room after room of treasures from different eras and styles.

Mid-Century Modern

Sleek lines, organic forms, and iconic design. Our vendors offer an impressive selection of mid-century furniture, lighting, and decor that defines cool sophistication.

Primitive & Farmhouse

Rustic charm meets timeless character. Weathered wood, galvanized metal, and homespun textiles. Perfect for creating that warm, lived-in farmhouse aesthetic.

Vintage Clothing & Jewelry

Fashion from decades past at amazing prices. Designer pieces, everyday wear, and stunning costume jewelry. Our clothing vendors handpick every item for style and quality.

Antique Books & Maps

First editions, rare volumes, vintage maps, and historical documents. Bibliophiles and history buffs will find treasures they never expected. Knowledge is beautiful.

Retro Electronics

Vintage radios, turntables, cameras, and more. Many items still functional and all are collectible. The perfect blend of technology nostalgia and industrial design.

Architectural Salvage

Vintage doors, stained glass, ironwork, hardware, and building materials. Perfect for restoration projects or adding character to new construction. History you can build with.

Oriental Rugs & Textiles

Handwoven rugs, tapestries, and textiles from around the world. Persian, Turkish, and Central Asian pieces with rich colors and intricate patterns. True works of art for your floor.

Where Every Visit Is
A New Adventure

Antique Depot is not just a shop. It is a destination. As Orange's largest antique mall, we bring together dozens of independent vendors under one roof, each with their own specialty, style, and pricing. That means you get the kind of variety and expertise that no single shop can match.

Our vendors are passionate about what they do. They hunt, curate, and price their own inventory, which means every booth reflects a personal vision and deep knowledge. You will find everything from high-end furniture to quirky collectibles, all under one roof.

Open every day of the week, Antique Depot makes it easy to shop on your schedule. And with new items arriving daily, there is always a reason to come back.
